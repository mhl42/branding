01 · Color

Three colors. That's the system.

Ink#0E1116

Primary. Strokes, type, dark backgrounds.

Bright background. Warmer than pure white — closer to a book page.

Single accent — the dot, accent rules, link underlines, callouts. Use sparingly.

08 · Usage

Do, don't.

Do

Use the mark with the phrase on first contact (web hero, deck cover, business card front), then drop the phrase for repeat appearances (favicon, app chrome, footer).

Don't

Don't recolor the mark with anything but ink, paper, or gold. Don't add a shadow. Don't put the gold dot anywhere except the top-right of the tile.

Do

Give the tile clear space equal to the height of the "42" caption on every side.

Don't

Don't stretch, rotate, or fill the tile interior. The tile is always an outline.

Do

Use the gold accent for one element per surface — the dot, a callout rule, or a link underline. Never multiple at once.

Don't

Don't typeset "Mostly Harmless" in italic or all-caps without the spaced-out tracking shown in this sheet.
